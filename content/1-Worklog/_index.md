**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Learn Module 01 and complete basic labs](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Learn VPC, Security, and VPN in Module 02](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Complete Labs 10, 19, and 20 in Module 02](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Learn Module 03, Module 04 about EC2 and storage services](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Practice Module 05 on Security and AWS KMS, IAM, Lambda, CloudWatch](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Learn Module 06 on Amazon RDS, DynamoDB, Lightsail, Auto Scaling, and ELB](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Review Modules 01-06 and research Data Lake on AWS](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Complete Module 07 on Analytics (Glue, DataBrew, Athena, QuickSight, Redshift)](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Finalize project topic "Automated Playwright Testing using Docker on AWS" and draft description](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Design architecture diagram for Playwright Automation Testing](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Optimize architecture, delegate tasks, and prepare resources for deployment](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Deploy, test, and troubleshoot the project system](1.12-week12/)
